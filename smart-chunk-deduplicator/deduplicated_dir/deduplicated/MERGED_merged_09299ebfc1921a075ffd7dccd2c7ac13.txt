--- Additional Information ---
## STATUTE 28B: HIGHER DOCTORATES 1. The University shall have powers to award a higher doctorate to a person who demonstrates distinguished published scholarly work and coherent contribution to knowledge in the relevant discipline. Any member of the University Council, the Academic Council or the Academic Board may propose in writing to the Vice-Chancellor a person to be considered for the award.

--- Additional Information ---
The procedure of summoning a Congregation for the presentation of graduands and for the conferment of degrees and awarding of diplomas and certificates shall be determined by the Academic Council in consultation with the Management Board.
