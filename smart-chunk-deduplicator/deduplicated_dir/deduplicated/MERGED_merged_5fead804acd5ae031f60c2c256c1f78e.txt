Students on academic leave are not registered and, therefore, do not have the rights and privileges of registered students. They cannot fulfill any official University requirements during the leave period. **Applying for Academic Leave** - A student can apply to take academic leave during their period of study of up to one year for on-going medical or personal reasons.

--- Additional Information ---
Personal reasons include, but are not limited to, financial reasons. - If a student wishes to apply for academic leave, they must make an application to the School Dean for approval. The application must include a description of the circumstances, together with any directly relevant evidence. - The cumulative period of academic leave that may be granted during an entire study period is one year.
