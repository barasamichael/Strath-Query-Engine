In addition to telecommunications and data network, the programme prepares candidates to handle systems security issues by training on the challenging task of identifying and delivering relevant solutions to threats that endanger information systems. In this regard, the candidates will be well prepared on systems security issues involving the analysis of computer systems - networks and applications - in order to assess their ability to protect against breach and disruption.

--- Additional Information ---
In addition to computer data networks, the programme prepares candidates to manage and secure linked computer systems and devices.
