Oduori **Lecturer** foduori@strathmore.edu **Profile** Dr. Fredrick N. Oduori is a lecturer in Tourism and Marketing at Strathmore University School of Tourism and Hospitality (STH). He joined STH as an adjunct faculty in Tourism, and holds the position of Lecturer from 2017 and Director of Research and innovation from 2019 to date.

--- Additional Information ---
Previously, he was assistant lecturer at the School of Tourism and Hospitality. Dr. Fredrick Oduori is passionate about leading his students to new and different perspectives on current and future business challenges in tourism and hospitality industry. He brings extensive management experience to his teaching Dr. Oduori consults as a Strategic Management and Marketing Practitioner.

--- Additional Information ---
He holds a Bachelor's degree in Tourism Management (Marketing) from USIU-Africa, MBA (Marketing) from University of Nairobi and MBA (International Business & Strategy) from USIU-Africa. His PhD is in Business Administration and Management from the University of Stellenbosch. His research interests include service marketing, competitive strategy and service innovation. Dr. Fredrick Oduori teaches business management and marketing, destination management and customer experience management.
