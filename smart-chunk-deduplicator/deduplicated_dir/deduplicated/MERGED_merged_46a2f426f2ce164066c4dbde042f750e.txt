As of 2025, Strathmore University joins a global network of 45 prestigious institutions that are committed to providing students with hands-on, project-based learning experiences across disciplines. #### What is VIP? Vertically Integrated Projects (VIP) is a multidisciplinary, multi-year, project-based learning experience that integrates students from various academic levels—undergraduate, graduate, and postdoctoral—into research teams.

--- Additional Information ---
These teams work on local and international research initiatives, often in collaboration with industry partners. VIP pdes students with an opportunity to contribute to long-term, high-impact projects while developing essential skills in leadership, teamwork, and research. At Strathmore University, VIP offers students an engaging and practical way to apply classroom knowledge in real-world contexts.
