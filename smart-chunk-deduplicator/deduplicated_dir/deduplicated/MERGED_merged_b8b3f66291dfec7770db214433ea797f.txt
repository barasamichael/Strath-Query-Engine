

--- MERGED CHUNK (from 6 sources) ---

Lecturers are strongly encouraged to provide a statement concerning academic integrity and a link to the Academic Integrity Policies of the University on their course outlines. - Students are responsible for understanding the principles of academic integrity and abiding by them in all aspects of their work at the University.

--- Additional Information ---
This implies that the lecturers not only provide the knowledge and training that prepare students to find their productive roles in society, but also help them discover and maintain integrity in the practice of that role. Staff and administrators are charged with representing the University accurately and forthrightly. Students are responsible for conducting their learning in a similarly honest and committed fashion.

--- Additional Information ---
- Lecturers, staff and administrators must deal fairly in all of their various decisions they make which have consequences for students and all the other stakeholders of the University. Students must strive to be fair to each other, for example in not claiming unjustified credit in carrying out joint projects and in the appropriate sharing of facilities. Staff must demonstrate impartiality in offering students information, opportunities and privileges.

--- Additional Information ---
- The University administration is responsible for working with lecturers and students to foster a strong institutional culture of academic integrity, for providing effective educational programs that create an understanding of and commitment to academic integrity and for establishing equitable and effective procedures to deal with allegations of violations of academic integrity.

--- Additional Information ---
- The lecturers share with the administration the responsibility for educating students about the importance and principles of academic integrity. They are expected to inform students of the particular requirements regarding academic integrity within their specific courses, to make reasonable efforts to minimize academic dishonesty, and to respond appropriately to violations of academic integrity.

--- Additional Information ---
Students are also encouraged to help educate fellow students about academic integrity and to bring all alleged violations of academic integrity they encounter to the attention of the appropriate authorities.
