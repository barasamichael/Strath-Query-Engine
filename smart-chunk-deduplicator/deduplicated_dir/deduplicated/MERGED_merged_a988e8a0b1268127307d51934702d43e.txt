If, after one of the brothers/sisters withdraws, only one brother/sister remains registered as a full-time student, he/she will no longer qualify for tuition fees benefits. --- Additional Information ---
4) Cessation of Fees Benefits As soon as one of the brothers/sisters ceases to be a student of the University, the other brothers/sisters studying on the Degree Programme will continue to enjoy tuition fees benefits, according to the scale shown above, provided that at least two brothers/sisters are still enrolled as full-time students.
