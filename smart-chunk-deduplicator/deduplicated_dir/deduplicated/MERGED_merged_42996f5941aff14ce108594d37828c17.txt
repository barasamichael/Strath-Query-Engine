**Leadership and professional engagements:** Dr. Kilavi has demonstrated strong leadership in the nuclear science community both in Kenya and internationally. She is the Chair of the OWSD-Kenya Chapter and has played a key role in advocating for inclusivity of women in nuclear science and engineering. She has mentored many girls through different programs including the TEWE-Energy project of the Faculty of Engineering, University of Nairobi.
