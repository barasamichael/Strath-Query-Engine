### Ethics and Theology Moral religious training is an essential part of the all-round education provided by Strathmore. Should any student not want to attend Theology lectures on grounds of conscience, he/she should inform the Chaplain. The Chaplain will arrange for the student to do a research paper on a specific topic instead.

--- Additional Information ---
This topic will be mutually agreed on and the Chaplain will make sure that he/she spends as much time in his/her religious training as attending the Theology lectures would require. ## STUDENT AFFAIRS Strathmore University views student life beyond the classroom as a means of fostering intellectual, spiritual, moral and social growth.
