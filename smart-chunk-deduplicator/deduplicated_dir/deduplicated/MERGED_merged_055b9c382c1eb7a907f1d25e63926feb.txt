# International Students Guide - Strathmore University ## Welcome to Strathmore University International Students Office Join our vibrant international community. With over 70 nationalities represented, our university is a place where the world comes together! We are here to support you throughout your journey and to help you make the most of your time here. Your one-stop office for a comfortable stay in Kenya.

--- Additional Information ---
We provide international students with information, assistance, and resources to help them adjust to life in Kenya and thrive while studying. Visit us at the Student Center 3rd Floor or contact us via studentpass@strathmore.edu to learn more. Our International students benefit from a streamlined visa application process supported by the university. While we cannot guarantee individual success rates, we strive to provide comprehensive guidance and assistance throughout the process.

## Welcoming Message from the Dean of Students On behalf of the Dean of Students Office, I extend a heartfelt welcome to all our international students! We are thrilled to have you join our vibrant and diverse community at Strathmore University. We understand that coming to a new country for your studies can be both exciting and challenging. You may be adjusting to a different culture, academic system, and way of life. The Dean of Students Office is here to support you in every step of the way.

--- Additional Information ---
Our office provides a wide range of programs and services designed to help you succeed academically and personally. We offer resources to help you navigate your studies, adjust to campus life, and connect with other students. Our goal is to ensure you feel welcome, supported, and empowered to thrive at Strathmore. We recognize the unique experiences and perspectives that international students bring to our campus.

--- Additional Information ---
By welcoming international students and fostering a sense of community, the office not only enriches the student experience but also strengthens Strathmore's global connections and contributes to the vibrant cultural landscape of the university and the world. On behalf of Strathmore University, we are thrilled to welcome you to Kenya!

--- Additional Information ---
The International Student Office is currently located within the Sports office on the 3rd Floor of the Student Centre. This one-stop office acts as a central hub for information, guidance and resources, assisting international students in adapting to life in Kenya and thriving at Strathmore.
