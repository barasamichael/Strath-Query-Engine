# Student Pass Application Requirements

## First-Time Applications

1. **Passport size photos** on a white background (Taken within the last six months)

2. **Copy of both bio data and a valid visa/stamp page from your passport**

3. **Copy of your basic academic transcripts and A/O level certificates**

4. **Equation of the basic academic certificates into the Kenyan system**
   - Visit: https://qa.knqa.go.ke/

5. **Commitment letter from parent/guardian** addressed to:
   The Director of Immigrations
   P.O. Box 30191‐00100
   Nairobi
   
   *Note: In the letter request for a student pass*

6. **Copy of parents'/guardians ID or passport**

7. **For self-sponsored students**, a certified copy of your Bank Statements is required

8. **Police clearance certificate/certificate of good conduct** from country of origin

9. **Proof of in-patient medical insurance** or a proof of purchase for the same

**Important**: To submit a request for the student pass, please use the following link: https://siso.strathmore.edu/

All documents must be valid at the time of submission.

---

## Renewals

1. **Passport size photos** on a white background (Taken within the last six months)

2. **Copy of both bio data and a valid visa/stamp page from your passport**

3. **Copy of your basic academic transcripts and A/O level certificates**

4. **Progress Reports/certificates** so far obtained from the university or initial institution

5. **Equation of the basic academic certificates into the Kenyan system**
   - Visit: https://qa.knqa.go.ke/

6. **Commitment letter from parent/guardian** addressed to:
   The Director of Immigrations
   P.O. Box 30191‐00100
   Nairobi
   
   *Note: In the letter, the parent/Guardian should request for a student pass on your behalf*

7. **Copy of parent's/guardians ID or passport**

8. **For self-sponsored students**, a certified copy of your Bank Statements is required

9. **Police clearance certificate locally acquired**
   - From DCI Headquarters, Kiambuu Road, Opp Karura Forest

10. **Proof of in-patient medical insurance** or a proof of purchase for the same

**Important**: To submit a request for the student pass, please use the following link: https://siso.strathmore.edu/

All documents must be valid at the time of submission.
