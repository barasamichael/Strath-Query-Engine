## Our Mission
We aim to provide you as a SU student or alumni with resources to prepare for industry and take charge of your career. Our services are anchored on the Transformative Student Experience pillar of the University's Strategy.

## About Career Services
The office was established circa 2002 to provide placement support to Accounting and Secretarial students and coordination of Attachments for the first degree cohort (BCOM & BBIT) in 2002.

In order for Schools to gain industry insights and feedback regarding our students, Attachment coordination was decentralized from our office to the various schools on campus in 2010. In 2011, we transitioned from Attachments Placement office to Career Development Services to reflect the shift in focus from a 'transactional office' to a transformative service.

We are located at Student center 3rd floor next to the International Students office.
